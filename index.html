Frequently Asked Questions

Discover Answers to questions you may have about our Plant-Based snacks

What are PlantFuel snacks?
PlantFuel snacks are high-protein, plant-based snacks made from wholesome ingredients like lentils and quinoa. They are designed for athletes and gym-goers who prioritize performance and sustainability. Our snacks contain no additives or preservatives, ensuring a clean and nutritious option.
Are they gluten-free?
Yes, all PlantFuel snacks are gluten-free. We use ingredients that are naturally gluten-free, making them suitable for those with gluten sensitivities. Enjoy our snacks without worry!
How are they packaged?
Our snacks are packaged in eco-friendly materials to minimize environmental impact. We believe in sustainability, not just in our ingredients but also in our packaging choices. Enjoy delicious snacks while supporting the planet!
What flavors are available?
PlantFuel offers a variety of flavors to satisfy different taste preferences. From savory to sweet, there's something for everyone. Check our product page for the full range of flavors!
Are they suitable for vegans?
Absolutely! All PlantFuel snacks are 100% vegan. We use only plant-based ingredients, making them a perfect choice for vegans and anyone looking to incorporate more plant power into their diet.
